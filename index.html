SHE RUN 2K19

Hope At Every Step

SHE RUN is proposed to be a district level women only mini marathon, a notion conceived by the women cell of GEC Palakkad as a token of women health awareness. While targeted policies and programs have enabled women to lead healthier lives, significant gender-based health disparities remain in many regions. Targeted to happen on World Health day, SHE RUN will feature a big section of women, ranging from students to kudumbasree members, to shape Palakkad’s first women only marathon.

Women health has always been a challenging issue, especially in rural areas. Not discounting the importance of health needs and health status of men, the fact remains that over a lifetime the health of women is usually worse than that of men. As students who represent a motive oriented women cell, we realise how mass and proper awareness can lead to a change in the current attitude. A mini marathon including women from different age groups and categories will serve this purpose in an effective way.
